WHAT IS THIS?


Nowadays there are not many therapists who specialize in children with special needs (Autism, Dawn syndrome, cerebral palsy etc.).

The conditions of their work are pretty terrible even if it is remote.

This enthusiastic student project is on a mission to become the most comfortable workspace ever for such specialists.

WHY IONKID?


FOCUS ON TREATMENT

No emojis. No ads. Keep it simple. Just you and your patients.

BLURRING THE BORDERS

Not everybody speaks English, so we want to save your time wasted on Google Translate. Read and write both treatment programs and messages in any language you prefer.

ALL IN ONE PLACE

Keep all your information on patients, treatment programs and to do lists in your workplace. Everything is secured.
